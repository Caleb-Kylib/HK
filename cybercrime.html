Fighting Cybercrime in Kenya

Key Provisions of the Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act

The rise of digital communication has increased cyber threats in Kenya. The Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act, 2018 is the cornerstone for addressing hacking, identity theft, and data breaches.

Scope of the Act

The Act addresses various digital offenses including unauthorized access, cyber espionage, harassment, and the dissemination of false publications.

Penalties

Offense Potential Penalty
Unauthorized Access Up to 5 years imprisonment or KES 5M fine
Cyber Espionage Up to 10 years imprisonment
False Publications Up to 2 years imprisonment
Important: Report any cybercrime incidents to the nearest police station or NC4 immediately.
