Human Rights Law

Advocating for justice, dignity, and the protection of constitutional rights for all Kenyans.

Human Rights in Kenya

The Constitution of Kenya, 2010 contains one of the most progressive Bills of Rights in the world. Human rights law in Kenya is about the practical enforcement of these rights, ensuring that the state and private entities respect the fundamental freedoms of every individual.

From the right to a fair hearing and freedom from discrimination to social and economic rights like the right to health and education, our firm is committed to high-impact litigation and individual advocacy. We help clients challenge administrative actions that violate their rights and seek redress through the Constitutional and Human Rights Division of the High Court.

Public Interest Litigation

We believe in using the law as a tool for systemic change. Our firm engages in public interest litigation to challenge laws or practices that are unconstitutional, helping to shape a more just and equitable legal environment for future generations.
