Human Rights Law

Advocating for justice, dignity, and the protection of constitutional rights for all Kenyans.

Human Rights in Kenya

The Constitution of Kenya, 2010 contains a progressive Bill of Rights. Human rights law enforces these protections against both state and private entities that violate fundamental freedoms.

This area covers the right to a fair hearing, freedom from discrimination, and social and economic rights. We pursue redress through the Constitutional and Human Rights Division of the High Court.

Public Interest Litigation

We use the law as a tool for systemic change, challenging unconstitutional laws or government practices to shape a more just legal environment for all Kenyans.
