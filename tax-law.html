Tax Law in Kenya

Taxation in Kenya is governed by a complex set of laws including the Income Tax Act, the Value Added Tax (VAT) Act, and the Tax Procedures Act. The Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) is increasingly utilizing technology and data analytics to enforce compliance, making expert legal advice essential for both individuals and corporations.

Navigating tax disputes requires more than just accounting; it requires legal strategy. From appealing tax assessments at the Tax Appeals Tribunal to advising on tax-efficient corporate structures, our firm provides a integrated approach to managing your tax legacy and ensuring you remain on the right side of the law while optimizing your financial position.

Tax Appeals Tribunal

If you disagree with a KRA assessment, you have the right to object and subsequently appeal to the Tax Appeals Tribunal. Strict timelines apply, and the burden of proof is often on the taxpayer. We handle the entire appeal process, from drafting to representation, to ensure your side is heard.
