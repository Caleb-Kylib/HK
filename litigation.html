Litigation in Kenya

The Kenyan legal system has undergone significant transformation, particularly with the implementation of the Civil Procedure Rules and the movement towards Court Annexed Mediation (CAM). Litigation is no longer just about the courtroom; it's about strategy and efficient resolution.

The High Court and various specialized courts (like the Employment and Labour Relations Court and the Environment and Land Court) have dedicated procedures. Our firm brings extensive expertise in navigating these hierarchies, from the Subordinate Courts up to the Supreme Court of Kenya, ensuring that our clients' cases are presented with the highest level of professional rigor.

Arbitration and Mediation

Under Article 159 of the Constitution, Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) is promoted as a means of improving access to justice. We are skilled in both Domestic and International Arbitration, providing a more private and often faster alternative to traditional court processes.
