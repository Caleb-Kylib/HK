Real Estate Law in Kenya

Real estate law is anchored in the Constitution, the Land Act, 2012, and the Land Registration Act, 2012. Given land's sensitivity in Kenya, rigorous legal expertise is essential to protect your investment.

The Ardhisasa platform has digitized land searches and transfers. Our firm conducts thorough due diligence to verify title, identify encumbrances, and ensure every transaction is legally sound.

Sectional Properties Act

The 2020 Act requires long-term apartment leases to convert to sectional titles, giving owners greater control and simplifying the management of shared spaces in multi-unit developments.
