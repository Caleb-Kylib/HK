Property in Kenya: Legal Insights

Understanding tenure systems and secure transactions.

Jul 15, 2025 Grace Njoroge

Investing in property in Kenya is a significant milestone. Understanding the legal landscape—from Freehold to Leasehold tenure—is crucial for every buyer and developer.

Types of Land Tenure

  • Freehold: Absolute ownership for an indefinite period.
  • Leasehold: Interest in land for a specific period (e.g., 99 years).
  • Community Land: Land held by communities on the basis of ethnicity or similar interest.

The Transfer Process

A typical property transfer involves a sale agreement, payment of stamp duty, and the final registration of the transfer at the Lands Registry. Our advocates ensure every step is handled with due diligence.
