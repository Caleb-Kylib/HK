Land Law

Modern Technology in Property Transactions

How digital registries, GIS mapping, and smart tools are revolutionizing property law in Kenya.

Jan 10, 2026 Tech Team

Key Insight

Modern property law relies on three core technologies: 1. Digital Registries (ArdhiSasa) for instant title verification; 2. GIS Mapping for boundary accuracy; and 3. Smart Contracts for secure, transparent transaction tracking.

Legal advocacy in property matters has evolved beyond traditional paperwork. At HakiLine, we leverage the latest digital tools to ensure your property rights are protected with precision.

1. Digital Registries & ArdhiSasa

The digitization of Kenya's land records has transformed how searches are conducted. We use the ArdhiSasa platform to verify ownership, check for caveats, and ensure that the title deed in your hand matches the official record in the clouds.

2. GIS Mapping & Boundary Verification

Large-scale parcels often have overlapping boundaries. We work with licensed surveyors who use high-altitude drone mapping and GNSS equipment to produce geo-referenced maps that integrate perfectly with legal documentation.

3. Ensuring Data Integrity

Investment in technology allows us to work faster and reduce human error. By using digital verification tools, we provide you with a secure legal audit trail that is essential for developers, lenders, and foreign investors.
