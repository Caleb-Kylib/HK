Find answers to common questions about our legal services

You can book a consultation by phone, email, or via the contact form on this site. For legal advice tailored to your matter, choose one of these options:
  • Phone: Call our office during business hours and our intake team will schedule a slot.
  • Online form: Complete the contact form with a short summary of your issue â€” we will respond within 48 business hours to propose available times.
  • Services page: Use the booking link on our Services page for consultations related to a specific practice area.
When booking please indicate whether you prefer an in-person meeting, phone call, or video consultation. We will confirm the appointment by email and advise on documents to bring.

Our principal practice areas include:
  • Corporate & Commercial Law: company formation, contracts, mergers & acquisitions, compliance.
  • Property & Conveyancing: sale/purchase support, title searches, transfers and related disputes.
  • Litigation & Dispute Resolution: civil litigation, arbitration, and alternative dispute resolution.
  • Family Law: divorce, custody, maintenance and related family matters.
  • Employment Law: contracts, disciplinary matters, redundancy and workplace disputes.
  • Immigration: visas, work permits and residency matters.
If your issue falls outside these areas we will try to refer you to a specialist who can assist.

Fee arrangements depend on the type and complexity of the matter. Typical options include:
  • Fixed fee: For transactional work such as simple conveyancing or drafting documents we may offer a fixed price.
  • Hourly rates: For litigation and complex advisory work we charge hourly rates which will be quoted in writing.
  • Retainer: For ongoing advisory relationships a retainer agreement may be used.
We usually offer an initial assessment meeting (which may be free or charged at a reduced fee depending on the matter). Before any work begins we provide a written engagement letter setting out scope, estimated costs and billing terms.

Response times depend on complexity. Our general targets are:
  • Routine enquiries: Response within 48 business hours.
  • Consultation booking: We will propose available slots within 1-2 business days.
  • Ongoing matters: We agree milestones and reporting cadence upfront; you will receive regular updates by your preferred method (phone, email, client portal).
If you have an urgent matter (statutory deadlines, court dates) please flag this when you contact us so we can prioritise appropriately.

We consider pro bono and reduced-fee assistance case by case, prioritising matters of significant public interest or vulnerability. To apply for assistance, please:
  1. Provide a brief summary of your matter and financial circumstances via the contact form or email.

Our intake team will review and advise whether we can assist directly or refer you to legal aid partners.

  • Card payments: We accept card payments via our secure payment provider for consultations and invoices.
Invoices include clear payment instructions and payment deadlines. For large matters we may require a retainer payment before work begins.
