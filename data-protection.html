Data Protection Laws in Kenya

Your rights and obligations under the 2019 Act — everything individuals and businesses need to know about data protection compliance in Kenya.

Aug 28, 2025 Peter Otieno

The Data Protection Act, 2019 governs the collection and processing of personal data in Kenya. Both businesses and individuals must understand these provisions to ensure compliance and protect privacy.

Key Data Subjects' Rights

  • Right to be informed of data collection.
  • Right to access personal data held by a controller.
  • Right to object to processing.
  • Right to correction of false or misleading data.
  • Right to erasure of data.

Business Obligations

Data controllers and processors must register with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) and implement robust security measures to prevent breaches.
