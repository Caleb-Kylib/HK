Understanding Contract Law in Kenya: A Practical Guide

By Jane Mwangi · Sep 12, 2025

This article explains the essentials of contract law in Kenya, including offer, acceptance, consideration, and remedies for breach. It provides practical advice for business owners and managers to draft stronger agreements and avoid disputes.

What makes a valid contract?

Valid contracts require intention to create legal relations, offer and acceptance, consideration and capacity. Written agreements help reduce ambiguity and provide clear evidence of terms.

Remedies for breach

When a party breaches, remedies may include specific performance, damages, or rescission depending on the nature of the breach.
