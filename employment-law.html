Employment & Labour Law

Navigating the relationship between employers and employees with legal precision.

Employment Law in Kenya

Employment law is regulated by the Employment Act, 2007, the Labour Relations Act, 2007, and the Labour Institutions Act, 2007 — establishing the framework for fair labour practices and workplace conflict resolution.

The Employment and Labour Relations Court (ELRC) has exclusive jurisdiction over labour matters. Recent trends include stricter enforcement of fair termination standards and growing emphasis on employee data privacy protection.

Unfair Dismissal & Procedure

Termination must be both substantively justified and procedurally fair, including a proper disciplinary hearing and the employee's right to accompaniment or representation.
