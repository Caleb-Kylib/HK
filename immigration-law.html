Immigration Law

Navigating Kenya's immigration policies for professionals, families, and investors.

Immigration in Kenya

Immigration is governed by the Kenya Citizenship and Immigration Act, 2011. Recent reforms include the eTA system and digitization of permit applications through e-FNS.

We advise on Class G investor permits, employment offers, and spouse residency. Our team assists with work permits, permanent residency, dual citizenship applications, and compliance with the Directorate of Immigration Services.

Investment & Permits

Class G investor permits require proof of at least US$100,000 in intended investment. We handle all documentation, application preparation, and follow-up with immigration authorities.
