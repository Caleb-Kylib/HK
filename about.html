The Firm

Our History

Founded in 2010 by visionary partners James Otieno and Sophia Njeri, HakiLine Advocates was established with a singular mission: to provide unparalleled legal representation rooted in integrity and excellence. What began as a boutique firm specializing in civil litigation has rapidly grown into a prominent, full-service legal institution.

Over the years, our founding partners were joined by esteemed legal minds, expanding our practice areas to corporate, commercial, and family law. Through unwavering dedication to justice, HakiLine Advocates has built a legacy of trust and a track record of landmark victories for businesses and individuals alike.
