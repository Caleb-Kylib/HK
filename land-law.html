How Kenya's New Land Law Changes Property Ownership in 2025

Navigating the 2025 reforms and legal procedures for secure property ownership.

Jul 15, 2025 Grace Njoroge

Land law in Kenya has undergone significant transformation. The transition to digital registration via ArdhiSasa is a key pillar of these reforms, aimed at securing property rights and reducing fraud.

The Land Act, 2012

This Act provides the legal framework for the management and administration of land. It classifies land into Public, Community, and Private land, each with distinct management protocols.

Digital Registration (ArdhiSasa)

The Ministry of Lands has digitized records to enhance efficiency. All land transactions, including searches, transfers, and charges, are gradually moving to this secure online portal.
